BATCH 07 — PILOT LAUNCH — USE AFTER THE APP WORKS

Launch as a consultant-led pilot first.

Pilot flow:
1. Client completes LimeSurvey.
2. You export responses from LimeSurvey.
3. You upload CSV/XLSX to the app.
4. App generates draft DOCX/PDF.
5. You manually review every report.
6. You send the PDF to the client.
7. You offer a review session or route repair sprint.

Do not launch full self-service until the app has passed at least 10 real/pilot reports without manual correction.
