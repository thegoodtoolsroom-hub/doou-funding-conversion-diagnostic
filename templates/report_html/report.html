Score Interpretation

Scores are presented as percentages (0–100%) and indicate readiness levels:

  • 80–100%: Strong readiness in this area; minimal barriers expected
  • 60–79%: Good foundation; targeted improvements can strengthen readiness
  • 40–59%: Moderate challenges; structured support recommended
  • 0–39%: Significant barriers; priority attention required
